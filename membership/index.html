JOIN US

Starting with the 2025/2026 season, all memberships, classes, and payments are managed through our fisofi page.

BECOME A MEMBER

Becoming a member is simple! Just follow these steps:

  1. Join our Telegram group.
    This is where we share the latest updates, announcements, and community news.
  2. Apply for membership via our fisofi page.
    Go to the Schedule section, fill out the membership form, and submit your application. Once approved, you'll be able to pay your membership fee directly through DanceCovery.
  3. Sign up for classes.
    After your membership is active, explore our class schedules and apply for the ones you'd like to join.

And that's it! You're ready to dance with us!
